Appointment Odoo App

This module helps businesses manage appointments with their customers or clients. It includes features such as appointment scheduling, booking, and confirmation, calendar integration, reminders, and the ability to assign staff members or resources to appointments.

Online Appointments

List of already created online appointments.

Online Appointment Creation

Have to provide Name for the appointment,Duration of the meeting.Can arrange the scheduling of the meeting according to the time taken for the appointment in the allotted space , canceling option in order to cancel the appointment in the provided time period and the Front end display can be set as no picture or show users pictures. Under the Availability Tab, you can provide the available days in a week for the appointment with the help of the Add a Line option.

Under the Options Tab, you will get some options, such as Timezone, Location, Limit to work hours option to trigger, create opportunities option to trigger, as well as Reminders to add in the allotted spaces. You can also send the appointment to whom you had set on the CC to option.

On the questions creation page, you can mention the questions in the provided space. The question type can be set as single line text, multi-line text, dropdown, radio, or checkboxes. You can provide the placeholder and trigger the required answer option if the answer to the question is necessary.

Appointment Publishing

If you want this appointment to get published on the website, just go for the Go To Website option.


On completing the publishing of the online appointments you will be provided with a share option.The share option helps you to share the link of the online appointment that you have created with the others in the company.


Reminder Configuration

Under the configuration option of the Calendar module, you will get the option of reminders, which helps you in setting alarms for the appointments, that can be online too.will get the calendar alarms from the reminders. The form includes the information, such as type, reminder before, and unit options.The type can be Email, SMS, or notification.


Reporting(analysis)

The online appointments and all other appointments will be available in this feature

Event

The created event.Have options for send mail or sms and video call consultation/meeting. Have option to give appointment fees and to create invoice for appointment fee. Direct access to invoices from form view.
